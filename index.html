CONNECTING THE FORTUNATE TO THE PEOPLE IN NEED IN YOUR CITY

Austin Giving Connection provides a way for people in need or in a crisis to receive help directly from other individuals.

How Does it Work

From each according to his ability,
to each according to his needs

Austin Giving Connection partners with local non-profits to find individuals who have the most critical needs and allows them to set up a profile that specifies their needs and tells their story.

Those who wish to donate can use the search function to find recipients and choose who receives their donation(s). This provides a more personal connection between the two and allows opportunities for relationships to develop.

Once recipients establish a profile, they can go in and update their information at any time. Profile input is stored in a real time database so that the donator can identify if the recipients' needs or location changes.

Who Can Participate

Eligibility - People in Need

Teenagers who are aging out of the foster care system

Chronically Homeless or Impoverished

Families who have experienced loss from natural disasters

Families or individuals in transition due to illness, death, or instability
